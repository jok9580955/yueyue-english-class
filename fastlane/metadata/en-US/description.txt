Yueyue's English Class is an early English learning app designed for children ages 2 to 6. It helps families build a gentle daily routine with short lessons, picture cards, listening practice, stories, and playful mini games.

The app organizes first words around familiar preschool themes, including animals, food, family, colors, toys, nature, body parts, and actions. Each card includes a clear visual, English pronunciation, Chinese meaning, and a simple prompt for parent-child practice.

Features:

- Age-based lessons for ages 2-3, 4-5, and 6
- Friendly topic packs for everyday first words
- Tap-to-listen English pronunciation
- Short story time for simple sentence exposure
- Mini games including bubble tap, shadow matching, rhythm tap, and letter train
- Learning records with stars, badges, daily paths, and weekly plans
- Parent center for child name, daily goal, gentle mode, and speech speed
- iPad-compatible split layout for shared learning

Yueyue's English Class focuses on interest, repetition, and parent-child companionship. Children can learn a little at a time while listening, tapping, matching, and speaking with a grown-up nearby.
